Michael Jordan

Jordan played 15 seasons in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Chicago BUlls and Washington Wizards. His biography on the NBA websites states: "By acclamation, Michael Jordan is the greatest basketball player of all time. Jordan was one of the most effectively marketed athletes of his gneration and was considered instrumental in popularing the NBA around the world in the 1980s and 1990s."
